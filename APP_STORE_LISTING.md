## Description
A premium booster pack opening simulator. Tear open booster packs from every era — the foundational early-2000s releases all the way to the latest premier sets. Era-accurate booster composition. Cards flip, shimmer, and glow based on rarity. Your collection lives on your device.

No ads. No subscriptions. Packs regenerate over time. Support the app with a one-time tip to unlock unlimited rips.

RIP PACKS FROM EVERY ERA
600+ sets bundled offline, organized by era — the original early-2000s releases, the mid-2000s spiritual successor era, the late-2000s expansion era, the early-2010s era, the mid-2010s era, the late-2010s era, and current premier sets. Pick a set, tear open a pack, see what you pull.

ERA-ACCURATE BOOSTER COMPOSITION
Pack composition is hand-authored per era to match real-world print runs. Old-school packs use the original 9-card structure with their era's rarity ratios. Modern packs use the current shape with realistic Ultra Rare, Secret Rare, and chase-rarity odds.

THE RIP IS EVERYTHING
Swipe to tear the foil wrapper. Tap to reveal your cards one at a time. Commons flip fast. Rares pause, glow, and hit harder. Chase-rarity pulls get a cinematic entrance — screen darkens, particles burst, shimmer sweeps across the card. Every pull feels different.

A METAL-SHADER FOIL SYSTEM
Cards shimmer with a real-time treatment that responds to motion. In Settings → Card Motion, choose Gyro (foil moves with your phone), Idle (gentle automatic sweep), or None. Tap and hold any card in the inspect view to tilt it manually in 3D. Honors iOS Reduce Motion.

TRACK YOUR COLLECTION
Every card you've pulled across every set. Grid view, binder view, list view. Sort by rarity, date, set, or value. Full set checklists show exactly what you're missing.

CARD VALUES
Live market prices on every card. See what your pulls are worth, sort by value, know which cards matter.

STATS & HISTORY
Pull history with timestamps. Rarity breakdown. Luckiest pulls. Set completion percentage. Watch your collection grow over time.

SUPPORT YGORIP - UNLOCK UNLIMITED RIPS
Three tip tiers, pick what feels right. Any tip unlocks unlimited pack opens — no waiting on the regen timer.

Every other feature — foil shimmer, particles, gyro tilt, full collection tracking, stats — is free for everyone. The tip is for unlimited access and to support development.

No subscriptions. One tip, yours forever.

YOUR COLLECTION STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE
Cards and pulls are stored locally. No accounts. No ads. No user tracking.

## App Review Notes (for Apple reviewer)

YGORip is a fan-made trading card pack opening simulator. It is not affiliated with Konami Digital Entertainment or any of its subsidiaries and contains explicit disclaimers in onboarding, the Settings screen, and the legal pages linked from Settings.

The app uses card metadata and card images sourced from YGOPRODeck (a long-established community API used by many third-party apps in this space). Its use is consistent with practices of other apps in this category.

No real cards are distributed or sold. All card pulls are simulated. The in-app tip tiers unlock unlimited pack-opening simulations and premium animations — they are tips to support the developer, not purchases of card content.

Card images are streamed and cached on-device; YGOPRODeck's terms forbid hotlinking and the app's image cache layer respects this by downloading each image once per device and persisting to disk.
